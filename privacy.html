Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what happens with your information when you visit therealtriune.com ("the site"), operated by UNO Ent. It's written in plain language rather than dense legal boilerplate, so it's easy to actually read.

The short version

This is a simple artist site. There's no account to create, no forms that collect your personal information, and no first-party analytics or advertising tracking installed by us. The only place cookies come into play is through the embedded Spotify and YouTube players in the Music and Content sections — and those are set by Spotify and YouTube, not by us.

Information we collect directly

We do not operate any sign-up forms, comment sections, or accounts on this site, so we don't directly collect names, email addresses, or other personal information through the site itself. If you email us using the address listed on the Contact page, we'll have whatever information you choose to include in that email, which we use only to respond to you.

Cookies from embedded content

When you click to play a release on the Music page or a video on the Content page, that section loads an embedded player directly from Spotify or YouTube (owned by Google). Those players may set their own cookies and collect information according to their own privacy policies, which are outside of our control:

  • Spotify Privacy Policy
  • Google/YouTube Privacy Policy

These embeds only load when you actively click to play something — they aren't loaded automatically just by visiting the page.

Do we sell or share your personal information?

No. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for money or other valuable consideration, and we have not done so in the past 12 months.

Your California privacy rights

If you're a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), gives you certain rights regarding personal information, including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to request deletion of personal information, the right to correct inaccurate personal information, the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

Since we don't directly collect or store personal information through the site itself, there is generally nothing on our end to request access to or deletion of. If you'd still like to make a request or have a question about your privacy rights, contact us using the details below and we'll respond.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your privacy rights can be sent to Triune@UNOEnt.com.
