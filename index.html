Bio

Triune is an independent recording artist whose career spans more than two decades. Known for thoughtful lyricism, soulful production, and a catalog that blends substance with timeless sound, he has built a loyal following while remaining completely independent.

“More Than 50 Television And Film Placements.”

His music has been featured in more than 50 television and film placements, including the theme song for CBS Sports' The Money Fight (Mayweather vs. McGregor) and ESPN's Year of the Black QB. He has also shared the stage with legendary artists including KRS-One, De La Soul, and Talib Kweli.

As an independent artist, Triune has generated more than 20 million television views, over 20 million streams, and more than 100,000 album sales across his catalog.

His latest project, The Green Tape, continues to earn national recognition. The standout record "Wiser Den Eva" was featured in Netflix's High on the Hog, an NAACP Image Award winning documentary series. With new music on the horizon, Triune continues to create records rooted in authenticity, purpose, and longevity.
